In the editor

Single-click a prompt to highlight it, double-click to fade it out. Drag prompts to reorder, and drag phrases up and down the bank the same way. Click ✎ to edit a prompt, and double-click any phrase to rewrite it. Five prompts is the maximum, laid out three then two.

Type a length straight into the timer (5, or 5:30) and press Enter to start it. A bar drains across the top of the screen as the time goes, amber under a minute and red once you're over.

Edits save as you type. Save to bank files what's on screen as a new debate and puts the one you opened back the way it was, so you can use an existing debate as a template.
